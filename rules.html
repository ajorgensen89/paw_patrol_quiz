Follow the rules to play the game...

  1. Enter a username
  2. Select a game level from 'easy', 'medium', 'hard'.
  3. Read the question and answer by clicking the box with the answer in that you think is the correct answer.
  4. The correct answer will light up in green.
  5. A wrong answer will light up red.
  6. See your score at the end.
  7. Enter any suggestions to add to the Quiz at the end of your game.
  8. Enjoy and play another level by returning to the 'level' page in the drop down menu to the right.
  9. If a new user wants to play the game, return to the main menu by clicking on the 'HOME' link on the top left of the page.
